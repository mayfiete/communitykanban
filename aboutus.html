About Us

Our Mission



Nonprofits

We are nonprofits that love our community. And we know what our community needs. We are driven to be the change that we want to see in the world. But so often technology holds us back. There's so much we can do, but we can't do it alone.



Community-Oriented Technologists

We are technologist that care deeply about our communities. So many of us want to help others, but don't know where to start. And as technologist, sometimes we are more comfortable creating things on our computer than connecting with people that we do not know. It's not that we are antisocial. But sometimes it takes us time to warm up to strangers.



Coming Together

We are nonprofits. We are technologists. We are the change.
