The process is simple!

Nonprofits

Step 1 Create a Login

Let's meet!

Step 2 Tell us what you are working on

What do you do? Tell us about your organization. We are deeply interested in understanding what makes you tick, and what you are doing to help the community.

Step 3 Create a Support Request

Go to the Nonprofit Support Page and create a support request. Our Community Kanban Project Manager will match you with the best technologist to help fulfill your request.

Step 4 Provide guidance as we work

The Community Kanban Project Manager will make sure you receive the best possible solution. You focus on your community. We focus on your technical needs.

Technologists

Step 1 Create a Login

Let's meet!

Step 2 Tell us about your Skillz!

What do you do? What are you interested in? Are you a web developer? A database engineer? PM? You will be matched with projects based on your skill set, experience and professional interest. There's a lot of work. There's always a way to help while building your professional experience.

Step 3 Go into the Community Kanban Marketplace

Explore relevant tasks. If you would like to work on a web development project, filter the list to only see tech requests related to website support. Do you want to build up your professional portfolio related to database engineering? Check if any nonprofits have reporting or data administrative needs. So many fun choices!

Step 4 Claim a Project

When you claim a ticket you have 7-days to verify that you will be able to complete the work. If you take on the work you will provide an estimate of how long it will take and any questions you may have. You can send your questions and comments in Community Kanban's slack channel. Let's Go!
